About the project

Have you ever been walking around your city and thinking by your self, wow what an amazing art! If not it would not suprise us, but anyway, it is a known fact that people feel really diffrently about grafiti. Some people hate it, while other like it. However, somitmes a person who does not like the graffiti calls in a complaint how much time does it take for the police to respond? Does it matter if it is a private or public proberty? Are many cases that are opend? what is the most likely place to observe graftiti? These are all questions we will try to answer.

Hower there are way to many citys to observe them all, so this time we will limit us to one of the hippiest citys in the world, The San Fransisco. The city offers many intresting sposts to preform art, for example the golden gate bridge, Alcatraz Island, Fisherman's Wharf and many more!
